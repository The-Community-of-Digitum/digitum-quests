# 🗂️ Quest Categories

Digitum recognizes the following quest categories:

- **Crafting Quests** — create items, recipes, or programs
- **Service Quests** — help another citizen
- **Repair Quests** — fix or improve something
- **Cultural Quests** — contribute to PCGs or culture
- **Exploration Quests** — discover or document something
- **Community Quests** — help newcomers or maintain systems

New categories may be added through PR.
